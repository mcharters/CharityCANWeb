What is this all about?

CharityCAN is Canada's premier prospect research, donor identification and relationship mapping platform. We created this interactive website as an inside look on some of the statistics that CharityCAN has collected for the top 1000 charities in Canada from 2017. At CharityCAN, we are guided by the idea that prospect research can be made more efficient through data. The datasets in CharityCAN are carefully selected to give our users the ability to find useful, meaningful, and accurate information about the people, companies, and charities they are researching.
