What is the Top 1000 Charity Report?

Every year, CharityCAN partners with The Globe and Mail to produce a ranking of the top 1000 non-profit organizations in Canada, based on public tax filings from each organization in the calendar year.

In previous years we ranked charities by total revenue, but for 2017 we've ranked them by the number of tax-receipted gifts received by each charity. That is, the number of donation dollars each charity has received where they issued a tax receipt. We thought this would give a better picture of the top charities working in fundraising today.

Scroll down to see where these top 1000 charities are located, some breakdowns in the data for these top charities, and a full ranking table of each charity in the list along with their position from last year. If you have any questions or comments, please get in touch!
